Our Mission

Leftovers For All is a community-powered platform focused on reducing food waste by helping neighbors share unused ingredients. Instead of throwing food away, we help it reach someone who needs it.

How It Works

1. Donate Ingredients

Post surplus pantry items, produce, or unopened groceries.

2. Request What You Need

Request ingredients to cook meals at home.

3. Match Locally

We connect people within the same neighborhood or zip code.

4. Exchange Safely

Pickup or drop-off is coordinated directly between users.

Donate Ingredients

  • Unopened pantry items
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Refrigerated or frozen ingredients (within safe dates)

No cooked or prepared meals.
